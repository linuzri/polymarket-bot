Automated Polymarket trading bot

Usage: polymarket-bot.exe <COMMAND>

Commands:
  markets    List active markets with volume and pricing
  market     Get detailed info about a specific market
  book       Get the order book for a market
  stream     Stream live price updates via WebSocket
  account    Show account balance and positions
  run        Run the automated strategy engine
  buy        Buy shares on a market (real order)
  sell       Sell shares on a market (real order)
  paper      Paper trading commands
  portfolio  Show real portfolio: open positions, resolved, P/L
  help       Print this message or the help of the given subcommand(s)

Options:
  -h, --help  Print help
